About Us

Welcome to Alice's Pancake and Crepes! We are a family-owned business dedicated to serving the finest pancakes and crepes in town. Our journey began with a passion for creating mouthwatering breakfast delights that bring joy to our customers' mornings. At Alice's Pancake and Crepes, we believe in using only the freshest, high-quality ingredients to craft our signature dishes. From classic buttermilk pancakes to savory crepes filled with a variety of delicious fillings, our menu offers something for everyone. Our commitment to quality extends beyond our ingredients. We take pride in providing exceptional service and a warm, welcoming atmosphere to all our guests. Whether you're joining us for breakfast with family or a quick bite with friends, we strive to make every visit a memorable one. We invite you to experience the taste of tradition and the comfort of homemade goodness at Alice's Pancake and Crepes. Join us for a delightful breakfast or brunch and let us make your day a little sweeter!
